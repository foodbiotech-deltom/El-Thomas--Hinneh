WHO I REALLY AM?

RESEARCH & ACADEMIC FOCUS: My academic and research interests include, Food Quality Control and Safety Systems, Postharvest Loss Reduction Nutritional Enhancement of Cassava-Based Products, Fermentation Technology, Food Processing & Preservation, Value Addition of Agricultural Commodities.
